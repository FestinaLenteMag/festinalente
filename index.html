Festina Lente Magazine

Festina Lente is a magazine that aims at discovering the different changes that have affected culture, in low but ongoing, neverending way.

The magazine takes into account two important fields: the museological environment and the world of books, literature and reading.

Every issue tries to focus on the transformation that the field has lived through digitization.

As a matter of fact, digitization has been crucial in these years: we have gone from books to e-books, from classic to virtual museums.

In every issue there are three articles that cover, in different aspects, the process of revolution in cultural field.

Three columns layout

The visualisation of the articles follows a specific layout - a three columns layout - so that the reader can read all the articles and compare them. In addition, the metadata viewer helps to compare the different types of metadata presented in each article.

Different styles to look at the revolution

Every issue of the magazine can be seen choosing different typographical styles. Every style is inspired by historical, sociological and cultural changes or movements. The styles you can find here are: XIX Century theme, Hippie theme, Hip-Hop theme, 2030 theme.
